**PURPOSE**

The structure `ZMM_PO_CLOSE_STRUC` is designed to support the custom report `ZMM_PO_CLOSE`, which is a utility for closing open purchase orders (POs). This report is intended to assist the purchase team in managing inventory, materials, and purchase planning by providing a comprehensive view of open POs and enabling bulk closure of these orders.

- **EBELN (Purchasing Document):** This field represents the unique identifier for each purchasing document, allowing users to track and manage specific POs.
- **EBELP (Purchasing Document Item):** This field specifies the item number within a purchasing document, enabling detailed tracking of individual items in a PO.
- **MATNR (Material):** The material number field identifies the specific material associated with the PO item, crucial for inventory and materials management.
- **TXZ01 (Material Description/Short Text):** Provides a brief description or short text for the material, aiding in quick identification and understanding of the material.
- **LIFNR (Vendor):** This field contains the vendor account number, linking the PO to the specific vendor supplying the materials.
- **NAME1 (Name):** Displays the name of the vendor, providing a human-readable reference to the vendor associated with the PO.
- **BEDAT (Document Date):** Indicates the date on which the purchasing document was created, useful for tracking the timeline of the order.
- **BSART (Document Type):** Specifies the type of purchasing document, which can be used to categorize and filter POs.
- **MENGE (PO Quantity):** Represents the quantity ordered in the PO, essential for understanding the scale of the order.
- **MEINS (Order Unit):** The unit of measure for the order quantity, ensuring clarity in the quantity ordered.
- **NETPR (Net Price):** Displays the net price per unit of measure, important for financial analysis and cost management.
- **NETWR (Net Value):** Shows the total net value of the PO item, providing insight into the financial commitment of the order.
- **WEMNG (Quantity Delivered):** Indicates the quantity that has already been delivered, helping to track fulfillment progress.
- **TO_BE_DEL (To Be Delivered):** Calculated as the difference between the ordered quantity and the delivered quantity, this field highlights the remaining quantity to be delivered, aiding in supply chain and delivery management.

Overall, this structure facilitates the display and management of open POs, allowing users to filter, sort, and close POs efficiently through the report's ALV grid interface.
